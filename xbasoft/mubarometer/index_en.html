Tips & tricks

Pressure/altitude indicators

For quick switching between pressure/altitude indicators just tap on the indicator icon.

You can measure the relative altitude. Just tap on the altitude indicator text (i.e. "Abs.") and it will show the relative altitude from the current point. Tap once more (on the "Rel." text) to return back to the absolute values.

The altitude value are based on the current pressure value. That's why it is not so accurate.

The data logger

To collect data μBarometer runs a small service which saves the pressure values.

Detailed graph

You can hide most of controls of the detailed graph by the button "▲".
